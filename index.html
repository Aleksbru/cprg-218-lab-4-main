FAQ

How many species of frogs are there?

+

In the amazon rainforest there are over 1000 kinds of frogs!!

What do they eat?

+

Insects like crickets, flies, grasshoppers, moths. If they're particularly hungry they might even eat a smaller frog!

What do they do all day?

+

They're usually swimming, trying to catch a bug with their tongue, or sleeping.
